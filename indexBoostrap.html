THE BOYS - KIMIKO

Kimiko Miyashiro, also known as The Female, is a fictional character in the Amazon Prime Video series The Boys, portrayed by Karen Fukuhara. Kimiko is a mute Japanese superhero who was forced to undergo experiments by Vought International that gave her superhuman strength, agility, and healing abilities. She is a member of The Boys, a group of vigilantes who take on corrupt superheroes.

THE BOYS - BLACK NOIR

Black Noir is a fictional character in the Amazon Prime Video series The Boys, portrayed by Nathan Mitchell. Black Noir is a mysterious superhero who is a member of The Seven, a group of superheroes who are marketed and controlled by Vought International. Black Noir is a silent and enigmatic figure who wears a black mask and suit that covers his entire body. He is known for his ruthlessness and his loyalty to Homelander, the leader of The Seven.
